Independent computational immunogenomics research conducted through Aletheia, identifying novel gene expression signatures in CD16+/- monocytes and CD4+ T cells for development of a Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE) diagnostic tool. Research contributed to ongoing lupus research at McMaster's Health Sciences Lupus Clinic.
